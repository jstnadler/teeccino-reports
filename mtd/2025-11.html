Net Revenue
$338,793
What is this?

The money we actually kept. Full reconciliation: DTC Gross - Line-Item Discounts = Subtotal; Subtotal + Shipping Revenue - Shipping Discounts - Refunds/Returns = Net Revenue. Subtotal-to-Net bridge has three pieces: shipping revenue collected, free-shipping promos applied, and post-order refunds/returns. This is the number that hits our bank account and the basis for the forecast comparison. See the Profit Waterfall tab of the Sales & Revenue Matrix for the full month-by-month breakdown.

How it's calculated

SUM(dtc_revenue) = Shopify currentTotalPrice (post-refund) for all DTC orders. Equivalent to: Subtotal + DTC Shipping Revenue (gross) - Shipping Discounts - Refunds & Returns.
