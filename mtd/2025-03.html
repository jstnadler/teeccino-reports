Net Revenue

What is this?

The money we actually kept. Reconciliation: DTC Gross - Discounts = Subtotal; Subtotal + Shipping Revenue - Refunds/Returns = Net Revenue. Shipping revenue collected from customers is included; refunds, returns, and post-order adjustments are subtracted. This is the number that hits our bank account and the basis for the forecast comparison.

How it's calculated

SUM(dtc_revenue) = Shopify currentTotalPrice (post-refund) for all DTC orders. Equivalent to: Subtotal + DTC Shipping Revenue - Refunds & Returns.
