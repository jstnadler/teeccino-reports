Definitions:
Subscription order = raw_orders.is_subscription = 1 (set during ingest from selling_plan_id on line items or order tags stay.ai / recharge / subscription / recurring).
First-time subscriber order = is_subscription AND customer_order_index = 1. Recurring = is_subscription AND customer_order_index > 1.
Sub-first cohort = month of a customer's FIRST-EVER order, where that order was a subscription. Excludes customers who started one-time and converted to sub later.
Active subscriber (proxy) = unique customer placing a sub order in the last N days. Best inferred = 90-day window (covers 30/60/90-day plans). We have no direct visibility into Shopify subscription contract status — paused/cancelled contracts simply stop appearing.
Wholesale excluded throughout. True Gross = qty × MSRP from price_list. Net = subtotal − total_discounts. Discount rate = sub_discounts ÷ sub_gross.
